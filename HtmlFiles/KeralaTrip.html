Hyderabad

The most places to visit near Hyderabad are Charminar,Golconda Fort,Ramoji Film City. Hyderabad with rich cultural heritage,showcases a varitey of traditional clothing like Hyderabadi sarees,khara Dupatta,Sherwani. The most widely used languages are Telugu and Urdu.Additionally,Englishe is widely used and understood,especially in urban areas and for business settings. Hyderabad boasts a vibrant culinary scene with several restaurants like Paradies,Bawarchi.Whether you're looking for luxury hotels,boutique stays, or budget-friendly options,the city has something for everyone like Taj Falaknuma Palace ,ITC Kakatiya, a Luxury Collection Hotel,Novotel Hyderabad Convention Centre.

KERALA

The most places to visit near Kerala are Munnar, Alleppey, Kovalam. Women and Men often wear a mix of traditional attire. Malayalam is the official language of Kerala and is widely spoken by the majority of the population. Hindi and Tamil are spoken by significant sections of the population. Kerala is known for its culinary tradition and can find a variety of restaurants offering delicious local and international cuisine.
