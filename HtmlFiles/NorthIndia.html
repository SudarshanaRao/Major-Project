North India Travel Guide

Delhi

The most places to visit near Delhi are Red Fort, India Gate, Lotus Temple. Delhiites often wear a mix of traditional and Western clothing such as saris, salwar kameez. Hindi is the official language of Delhi and is widely spoken by the majority of the population. Urdu and Punjabi are spoken by significant sections of the population, particularly in Old Delhi and areas with a concentration of Punjabi communities. Delhi is a culinary haven with a diverse range of restaurants offering everything from traditional Indian cuisine to international flavors. Here are some famous and popular restaurants in Delhi such as Indian Accent, Bukhara, Dramz many more.

Varanasi

The most places to visit near Varanasi are Kashi Vishwanath temple, Sarnath, Ramnagar Fort. Varanasi is considered one of the holiest cities in Hinduism. Hindi is the official language of Delhi and is widely spoken by the majority of the population. Bhojpuri and sanskrit are spoken by significant sections of the population. Varanasi is a culinary haven with a diverse range of restaurants offering everything from traditional Indian cuisine to international flavors. Here are some famous and popular restaurants in Delhi such as Varanasi tea villa cafe,brown bread bakery and more.
